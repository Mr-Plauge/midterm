About Me

Hello! I am Owyn Lee, an aspiring game designer from California. I have been designing assets and programming for games since I was 11. I published my first mod, MrPlague's Authentic Races, at the age of 13. This portfolio contains an overview of my projects, the design process behind each of them, and a showcase of the assets made for them.
